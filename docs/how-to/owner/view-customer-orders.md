# View Wholesale Customer Orders

1. Navigate to **Wholesale Customers** page
2. Click the **Lihat Pesanan** button on the customer row
3. A modal will display all orders for that customer
4. Orders show: invoice number, date, total, status
5. Click outside the modal or press Esc to close
