# Wholesale Business Model

## Why Wholesale?

The core business sells perfumes at retail (eceran) in physical stores. The wholesale channel (grosir) addresses B2B customers — resellers, event organizers, and Islamic boarding schools (pesantren) that buy in bulk.

## Wholesale vs Retail

| Aspect | Retail | Wholesale |
|---|---|---|
| Customer | Walk-in | Registered B2B |
| Pricing | Fixed retail price | Tiered wholesale price |
| Payment | Instant (cash/QR) | Manual transfer, term |
| Fulfillment | Take-away | Shipped via courier |
| Return | Immediate | 3-day inspection period |
| Volume | Single units | Bulk (minimum order) |

## Wholesale Customer Lifecycle

1. **Registration** — Customer registers via portal or owner creates manually
2. **Verification** — Owner verifies business legitimacy
3. **First Purchase** — Customer places first order
4. **Loyalty Building** — Each purchase builds credit toward higher tiers
5. **Tier Upgrade** — Higher tiers unlock better wholesale prices

## Why Soft Deletes?

Wholesale customers might stop ordering but their transaction history must remain accessible for accounting and tax purposes. Soft deletion preserves records while removing the customer from active views.
