# Wholesale Loyalty & Credit System

## Why a Loyalty System?

Wholesale customers are the business's highest-value segment. Retaining them is more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. The loyalty system incentivizes repeat purchases through tier-based pricing.

## Tier Mechanics

| Tier | Minimum Purchase (Monthly) | Discount |
|---|---|---|
| Bronze | 0 | Base wholesale price |
| Silver | Rp 5,000,000 | 2% additional discount |
| Gold | Rp 15,000,000 | 5% additional discount |
| Platinum | Rp 30,000,000 | 10% additional discount |

## Credit (Hutang) System

Wholesale customers may be extended credit — the ability to place orders and pay later. This is tracked at the customer level, not per-order. Key rules:

- Credit limit is set manually by the owner per customer
- Outstanding balance cannot exceed the credit limit
- New orders are blocked if balance exceeds limit
- Payment reduces the outstanding balance
- Interest is not charged (religious compliance)

## Why Credit Instead of Payment Gateway?

Payment gateways charge fees (2-4% per transaction) which eat into wholesale margins. Manual transfer with credit tracking gives:
- Zero transaction fees
- Flexibility for trusted customers
- Cash flow predictability
- Shariah-compliant (no riba/interest)
